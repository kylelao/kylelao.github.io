The assignment was to make a sonnet about your actual life and how it relates to yourself. Figuring out words to use for the sonnet was the hardest. How to Rhyme related words. This was fun because you can do anything to it to make it better and just change it.

The Struggle

I wake up each day that is on repeat
These days I put my journey on shuffle
In my heart their is a consistent beat
I have all my problems in the duffle

Hiding behind in shadows from others
Can’t compete against the light that loosens
I’m always slipping through life like butter
Everyone in my class makes me stupid

Realizing life is reality
Changing it upside down and inside out
Wants someone to keep straight with the referee
Need to know what life is really about

Soon I will know what's right with my blind eyes
Only thing I see is beautiful skies


Lao, 2017
